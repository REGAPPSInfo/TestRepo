Overview of PDFAssist

PDFAssist from REGAPPS, is a comprehensive Windows application that empowers you to view, edit, prepare, audit, and validate PDF documents with ease. Designed specifically for the regulatory landscape, it offers an innovative approach to meet the requirements of both electronic Common Technical Document (eCTD) submissions and non-eCTD electronic submissions (NeeS).

PDFAssist eliminates the need for Adobe Acrobat by offering a built-in PDF viewer and a suite of specialized add-on tools. Each tool is purpose-built to support eCTD and NeeS regulatory compliance requirements for the life sciences industry, addressing specific submission needs in alignment with global agency standards and industry best practices.

PDFAssist is designed to support submission publishers and reviewers in preparing, publishing, and delivering PDF documents that fully comply with global regulatory guidelines.

Why PDFAssist? How PDFAssist solution is different than what you currently use from other vendors?

Most of the PDF solutions developed for desktops are Adobe based with additional plug-ins. You are required to buy two licenses separately one for Adobe and one for Plug-ins for a single user where as for PDFAssist one license suffice both, since this solution has viewing, editing and inclusive of PDF tools.

The main idea behind the developing this solution is to have all-in-one PDF solution i.e. Viewing, editing and PDF tools to assist submission publishing.

The team at REGAPPS has significant technical experience and knowledge of PDF specifications. They have gained this knowledge by developing PDF solutions using various PDF SDKs and most important, team is expertise in regulatory domain.

The architects of PDFAssist has over 30 years of combined experience in developing automated PDF solutions.

Most important, team has good understanding of regulatory domain specifications and good knowledge in eCTD/NeeS regulatory requirements from the agencies around the world.

Desktop solution that doesn’t require Adobe, rich user interface,speed and built-in tools makes PDFAssist the reliable and powerful.
