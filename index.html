REGAPPS Product Portfolio

PDFAssist

Introducing PDFAssist, a unified PDF solution for life sciences regulatory operation professionals. This application enables seamless viewing, editing, and preparation of PDF documents. Equipped with specialized add-on tools, it simplifies the compilation process to ensure full compliance with ICH, eCTD and NeeS regulatory standards.

eCTDValidator

eCTDValidator, seamlessly integrated with ViewerPlus, is a robust validation tool designed to ensure technical compliance with global health authority standards. Supporting both eCTD and NeeS formats, it rigorously checks submissions against region-specific rules and guidelines—delivering accurate, consistent, and submission-ready outputs with confidence

ViewerPlus

Unified Viewing & Validation for eCTD Submissions. ViewerPlus is a Windows-based application purpose-built for efficient viewing and validation of eCTD and non-eCTD dossier formats. With its intuitive interface and high-speed performance, it streamlines the lifecycle management of electronic submissions-delivering a fast, user-friendly experience and seamless navigation between viewer and validator functions.
